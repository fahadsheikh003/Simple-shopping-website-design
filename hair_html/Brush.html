The Double Shot Blow-Dryer Brush

A GH Beauty Award winner, this inventive 2-in-1 Drybar hair styling tool combines a blowdryer and a hairbrush in one wand that dries strands as you brush through. The top-ranked hair dryer brush was “comfortable to hold” and glided smoothly through hair, testers reported, and it didn't overheat the hair or scalp. “I loved it!” one raved.
